# DailyAI Go-To-Market Plan (Germany + International)

## 1) Positioning (Pitch-Ready)

DailyAI is the fastest way for professionals to turn AI news into action.

- Core promise: "From AI headlines to decisions in 60 seconds"
- Target user: founders, product teams, developers, marketers, students
- Wedge: personalized, multilingual AI-news decisions with confidence + impact framing
- Differentiator: local language + country feed + role-based next-step guidance

## 2) Product-Market Fit Checklist

### Must-Have User Outcomes

- User can scan 10+ updates in under 2 minutes
- User can save and revisit important signals quickly
- User gets a clear next action from each story
- User sees relevant content improve within 3 sessions

### PMF Metrics to Track Weekly

- D1 retention: target >= 30%
- D7 retention: target >= 12%
- Save rate per session: target >= 15%
- Stories opened per session: target >= 6
- Brief generation success rate: target >= 98%
- Install prompt acceptance (PWA): target >= 8%

## 3) Germany-First Launch Strategy

### Localization

- Keep German copy human-edited for landing text and onboarding
- Add Germany-specific source weighting for business + regulation topics
- Add "DACH" region quick-filter in roadmap (DE/AT/CH)

### Compliance and Trust (Critical for Germany)

- Add legal pages: Impressum, Datenschutzerklaerung, Terms
- Confirm consent flow for analytics and local storage
- Document data processing purpose and retention windows
- Show source transparency in every card/sheet

### Distribution Channels

- Launch in German AI founder and developer communities
- Post weekly "AI Decision Brief" threads on LinkedIn/X
- Partner with 3-5 German AI newsletters/podcasts for co-promo
- Offer "Team Digest Beta" for startups and agencies

## 4) International Expansion Playbook

### Wave 1 (English + German)

- Focus countries: DE, US, UK, IN
- Keep one shared product narrative and local examples per market
- Run creator-led distribution with weekly benchmarks

### Wave 2

- Add localized onboarding variants by role + region
- Introduce referral loops (share card -> open app -> save)
- Launch "DailyAI Pro" waitlist (alerts, sector feeds, team workspace)

## 5) Pitch Deck Structure (10 slides)

1. Problem: AI news overload, low signal-to-noise
2. Solution: decision-first news in 60 seconds
3. Product demo: feed -> brief -> save -> related discovery
4. Why now: AI velocity + multi-market demand
5. Market: Germany wedge, global expansion TAM
6. Traction: retention, saves, session depth, subscribers
7. Moat: personalization graph + multilingual decision UX
8. Go-to-market: Germany first, community + creator channels
9. Business model: free + pro + B2B digest seats
10. Ask: funding, intros, pilot design partners

## 6) 30-60-90 Day Execution Plan

### First 30 Days

- Instrument key product metrics dashboard
- Ship legal/compliance pages for Germany
- Recruit 50-100 German beta users
- Run weekly product interview cadence (>= 8 interviews)

### Day 31-60

- Improve ranking based on saves/taps/reads
- Launch weekly changelog and roadmap page
- Add referral + share attribution tracking
- Start founder-led content rhythm (3 posts/week)

### Day 61-90

- Launch Pro waitlist and paid design partner program
- Run 2 growth experiments per week
- Expand to 2 additional high-intent regions
- Prepare investor update with retention cohort proof

## 7) Missing Pieces to Build Next

- Event analytics pipeline (Mixpanel/PostHog/Amplitude)
- A/B testing framework for onboarding and ranking
- Email digest v1 with segmented topics and role
- Source quality scoring visibility in UI
- Faster article summarization fallback when LLM call fails
- Error budget and SLO dashboard for feed freshness

## 8) Suggested Pitch One-Liner

"DailyAI helps busy professionals turn the AI news firehose into clear, personalized decisions in 60 seconds, starting with Germany and scaling globally through multilingual, action-first discovery."
